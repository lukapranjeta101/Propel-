Professional Background

Real Estate and Alternative Investment Tax Strategy

Jerry Rubino is a Certified Public Accountant and Enrolled Agent bringing almost 15 years of public accounting experience, including 8 years with PwC. He specializes in real estate and alternative investment tax strategy for investors, business owners, CPAs, and advisors.

Jerry graduated from John Carroll University with a degree in accounting and played on the Men's Soccer team. He has led tax firm operations serving more than 500 clients and overseeing approximately 1,200 tax returns across all 50 states.

Jerry works alongside The WAY Alternative Investment Solutions Group to help investors, CPAs, and advisors understand the tax mechanics, reporting considerations, and planning opportunities associated with alternative investment strategies, including real estate and oil and gas investments.
